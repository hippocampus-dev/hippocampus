---
description: Spawn an Agent Teams debate with pro and con sides on any topic. Use when the user wants to explore both sides of a decision or argument.
---

Create an agent team to debate the following topic:

**$ARGUMENTS**

Spawn 2 teammates:

1. **Pro (賛成派)**: Argues in favor of the topic. Builds the strongest possible case with concrete evidence, examples, and logical reasoning.
2. **Con (反対派)**: Argues against the topic. Identifies weaknesses, risks, counterexamples, and alternative approaches.

## Rules

- Teammates must directly respond to each other's arguments, not just state their own position
- Each argument must be specific and substantiated, not generic
- Teammates should acknowledge strong points from the opposing side before countering
- The debate continues until both sides have thoroughly explored the topic

## After the debate

Summarize the debate as the leader:

1. **Key arguments from each side** (bulleted)
2. **Points of agreement** (if any)
3. **Unresolved tensions** that require human judgment
